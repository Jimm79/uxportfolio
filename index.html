• CASE STUDIES •

Bounce Video

Contemporary media streaming platforms are rich in content, but that is offset by their seeming lack of empathy for the user's need to express themselves or zero in on what their heart desires. Bounce aims to remedy that by implementing a revolutionary metadata-based filtering system, and incorporating functions to review, rate, and discuss movies and TV shows.

XPlore

Navigation apps should be more than just about getting you to your destination. They should also be about how they get you there! For every motorist who has been forced to put up with the vagaries of our current navigation applications, here's an alternative so intuitive and efficient that you'll think it's from the future!

Paws A Minnit

It's the age of pet influencers, and some like Jiff the Pomeranian command over 9.5 million followers on social media. What they lack, however, is a virtual platform they can call their own. Paws A Minnit aims to bridge that gap by becoming a one-stop destination for everthing related to pets, whether it's watching videos, seeking advice, or shopping for products.
